EN: What is backward chaining? Backward chaining starts from a goal and works backward through rules to find facts that prove it. It is how Winter AI resolves intent queries.
FR: Qu'est-ce que le chainage arriere? Le chainage arriere part d'un objectif et remonte a travers les regles pour trouver les faits qui le prouvent.
RW: Backward chaining ni iki? Bitangira ku ntego bakajya inyuma mu mategeko kugira ngo babone ibihamya byayo.

EN: What is TF-IDF? TF-IDF stands for term frequency-inverse document frequency. It scores how important a word is to a document relative to the whole knowledge base.
FR: Qu'est-ce que TF-IDF? TF-IDF signifie frequence des termes multipliee par la frequence inverse des documents.
RW: TF-IDF ni iki? Ni uburyo bwo gupima akamaro k'ijambo muri dosiye imwe ugereranije n'ubumenyi bwose.

EN: What is Levenshtein distance? It counts the minimum number of single-character edits to turn one string into another. Winter AI uses it as a fuzzy fallback matcher.
FR: Qu'est-ce que la distance de Levenshtein? Elle compte le nombre minimum de modifications d'un seul caractere pour transformer une chaine en une autre.
RW: Intera ya Levenshtein ni iki? Ibara umubare muto wa guhindura inyuguti imwe ku guhindura ijambo rimwe guturuka aho rindi.
